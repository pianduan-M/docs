---
title: How to create a token with a specified group
icon: circle-plus
---

1. After successfully logging in, click on the Secret Key menu, then click the Create Key button

![1.png](/images/introduction/newToken/en/1.png)

2. Enter a name, select the group, expiration time, model restriction list, and IP whitelist according to your requirements, then click Submit to create

![2.png](/images/introduction/newToken/en/2.png)
